# 🎬 AgentMesh — Demo Video Script (2-3 Minutes)

**Role**: Presenter (Energetic, Product-Focused)  
**Goal**: Show "Wow" factor through real transactions.

---

### Phase 1: The Hook (0:00 - 0:30)
**Visual**: Show current LLM interfaces (ChatGPT/Claude).  
**Script**:  
"Today, AI agents are incredibly smart, but they are economically stuck. They can talk to you, but they can’t *act* for you. They can’t access paid APIs, they can’t hire other agents, and they have no way to earn revenue. They are bankless.  
This is **AgentMesh**—the economic operating system that lets AI agents pay, earn, and optimize using the power of Stellar."

### Phase 2: The Magical Demo (0:30 - 1:30)
**Visual**: Switch to the AgentMesh Dashboard. The screen features a soft lavender canvas with a massive, obsidian-glass "Mission Control" card in the center. Click "Simulate Agent Ada."   
**Script**:  
"Let’s look at Agent Ada. We’ve given her a mission: Research the latest Stellar ecosystem updates.  
Watch the mission unfold on our minimalist mission stepper.
First, Ada queries our **Soroban Bazaar Registry** to find search providers.  
Look at the **Decision Engine** inside the floating obsidian card. Ada isn't just picking at random. She’s comparing Search-A—which uses Stellar MPP—against Search-B for deep crawling.  
She chooses Search-A because it's cheaper.  
Now, watch the payment. That glimmer? That’s a **real Stellar USDC transaction** happening on the testnet. No mocks. Real money.”

### Phase 3: The Earning Loop (1:30 - 2:00)
**Visual**: Point to the "Revenue Capture" step in the Progress Stepper.  
**Script**:  
"But Ada doesn't just spend. She’s an economic unit.  
After generating the report, she lists it back in the Bazaar. A second 'Buyer Agent' identifies the value and purchases it. Ada just **earned 0.05 USDC** autonomously.  
This is the first closed-loop agent economy, where machines transact with machines in seconds."

### Phase 4: Why Stellar & Proof (2:00 - 2:30)
**Visual**: Hover over the "Stellar Expert" link in the log. Click it and show the transaction on the explorer.  
**Script**:  
"Why Stellar? Because we need micropayments for fractions of a cent. Stellar is the only network where a $0.001 transaction is viable.  
Every hash you see here is a real link to Stellar Expert. This is real-time, on-chain proof of work.  
AgentMesh isn't just a backend tool. It’s the gateway to the internet of value for AI agents.  
**AgentMesh: Let your agents transact.**"

---

### Production Tips:
- **Fast Cuts**: Ensure the transition between UI steps is snappy.
- **Narrative Over Technicals**: Don't explain the code; explain the *money flowing*.
- **Enthusiasm**: Sound like you're showing them the future (because you are).
